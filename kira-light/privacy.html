隐私政策 — Kira补光灯

最后更新：2026年4月28日

概述

Kira补光灯（以下简称"本应用"）尊重并保护用户隐私。本应用是一款纯本地工具，不收集、不上传、不共享任何个人信息。所有补光效果与预设均在设备本地实时处理，应用不进行任何网络请求。

权限说明

本应用可能请求以下系统权限，均为可选功能所需：

  • 相机（Camera）：用于「自拍补光」功能，在屏幕补光的同时提供前置摄像头实时预览与拍照。相机画面仅在您的设备本地显示和处理，不会上传至任何服务器。
  • 麦克风（Microphone）：本应用的自拍补光功能使用实时取景技术（LivePusher），该技术在系统层面需要声明麦克风权限。但本应用不会录制、存储或传输任何音频数据，也不会主动开启麦克风。
  • 相册写入（Photo Library — Add Only）：用于将您在「自拍补光」模式下拍摄的照片保存到系统相册。应用仅请求"添加照片"权限，不会读取或访问您相册中的其他照片。

如果您不使用自拍补光功能，应用的核心补光功能（屏幕作为补光源）无需任何权限即可正常使用。

数据收集

本应用不收集任何个人身份信息。所有使用数据（如亮度偏好、预设设置）均存储在您的设备本地，不会传输到任何服务器。

第三方服务

本应用不向第三方传输您的数据，也不集成第三方分析或广告 SDK。

应用内购买

您可选择一次性付费解锁 Pro 功能，包括更多预设与自定义预设。交易由 Apple 处理；我们不会收到您的完整支付卡信息。

儿童隐私

本应用并非面向 13 岁以下儿童设计或营销。我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请发送邮件至 kapps.dev@outlook.com

Privacy Policy — Kira Fill Light

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Overview

Kira Fill Light ("the App") respects and protects user privacy. This is a local-only tool that does not collect, upload, or share any personal information. All fill-light effects and presets are processed locally on your device in real time. The app does not make network requests.

Permissions

The app may request the following system permissions, all for optional features:

  • Camera: Used for the "Selfie Lighting" feature, which provides a live front-camera preview and photo capture while the screen serves as a fill light. Camera feed is displayed and processed entirely on your device and is never uploaded to any server.
  • Microphone: The selfie lighting feature uses live camera preview technology (LivePusher) that requires microphone permission to be declared at the system level. However, this app does not record, store, or transmit any audio data, and does not actively activate the microphone.
  • Photo Library — Add Only: Used to save photos taken in the "Selfie Lighting" mode to your system photo library. The app only requests "Add Photos" permission and does not read or access any other photos in your library.

If you do not use the Selfie Lighting feature, the core fill-light functionality (screen as a light source) works without any permissions.

Data Collection

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information. All usage data (brightness preferences, preset settings) is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

We do not transmit your data to third parties, and we do not integrate third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

In-App Purchase

You may make a one-time purchase to unlock Pro features, including more presets and custom presets. Payments are processed by Apple; we do not receive your full payment card details.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions, email kapps.dev@outlook.com
