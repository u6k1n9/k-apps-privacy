常见问题

补光不够亮怎么办？
请将系统亮度调高，并在应用内选择较高亮度的预设；录制视频时也可在系统相机中配合使用。

Pro 版包含什么？
一次性 ¥3 解锁更多色温预设与自定义预设功能。

需要联网吗？
不需要。所有处理均在设备本地完成。

已购买 Pro 但功能未解锁？
请在「设置」中轻点「恢复购买」，并确认使用购买时所用的 Apple ID。

FAQ

The light doesn’t seem bright enough.
Raise system brightness and choose a brighter preset in the app; you can also use it alongside the system camera when recording.

What’s included in Pro?
A one-time ¥3 purchase unlocks more color-temperature presets and custom presets.

Does the app need internet?
No. Everything runs on your device.

I bought Pro but features are still locked.
Tap “Restore Purchases” in Settings and confirm you’re signed in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase.
