FAQ

What happens if I forget my passcode?
Your vault is secured on-device. If you forget your passcode, we cannot reset it or recover your photos—there is no back door and no cloud copy. You may need to delete and reinstall the app, which removes locally stored vault data. Back up anything important outside the vault using means you control.

What is a decoy password?
With Pro, you can set a secondary password that opens a decoy vault—typically shown as empty or separate from your real content—so you can unlock the app without revealing your private albums. Configure it in the app’s security settings.

Are my photos backed up?
PhotoVault does not upload your photos to our servers. Whether copies exist elsewhere depends on your device settings (for example, iCloud Photos, Finder/iTunes backups). Items only inside the app’s sandbox are not backed up to our cloud; Apple’s backup behavior is described in Apple’s support documentation.

How does Face ID / Touch ID work?
Biometrics are handled by iOS through LocalAuthentication. We never receive your face or fingerprint data—only a success or failure result from the system.

I bought Pro but features are still locked.
Use Restore Purchases in the app and confirm you are on the same Apple ID used for the purchase.
