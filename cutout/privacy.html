✂️ 秒抠

CutOut - Background Remover

Last updated: March 27, 2026

数据收集

秒抠（CutOut）不会在服务器上收集或存储您的照片。抠图与背景替换均通过 Apple Vision 框架在设备本地完成，图片不会上传至我们的系统。

权限说明

为从相册选取图片并将处理结果保存回相册，本应用会请求「照片」相关权限（读取与保存）。我们仅用于您主动选择的图片操作，不会在后台扫描您的整个图库。

第三方服务

除 Apple 系统框架（Vision、Photos）外，本应用未集成向第三方传输您图像数据的分析或云服务。

应用内购买

您可选择一次性付费 ¥3 解锁 Pro，以享受不限次数抠图、全部背景模板与高清导出等。付款由 Apple 通过 App Store 处理。

儿童隐私

本应用并非面向 13 岁以下儿童设计，我们亦不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

数据分析

本应用不包含任何分析或统计追踪功能。

联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请发送邮件至：kapps.dev@outlook.com

English Version

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Data Collection

CutOut does not collect or store your photos on our servers. Background removal and replacement run on-device using Apple’s Vision framework; images are not uploaded to us.

Permissions

To import images from your library and save results, the app requests Photo Library access (read/save). We use this only for actions you initiate; we do not scan your entire library in the background.

Third-Party Services

Beyond Apple system frameworks (Vision, Photos), we do not integrate third-party services that transmit your images for analytics or cloud processing.

In-App Purchase

You may optionally purchase Pro for a one-time fee of ¥3 (or local equivalent) for unlimited cutouts, all background templates, and HD export. Payments are processed by Apple via the App Store.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Analytics

This app does not include analytics or tracking.

Contact

For privacy questions, email kapps.dev@outlook.com
