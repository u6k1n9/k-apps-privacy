秒抠如何去除背景？

用于导入您选择的图片以及将结果保存到相册。

Pro 有什么区别？
一次性 ¥3 可解锁不限次数抠图、全部背景与高清导出（以应用内说明为准）。

FAQ

How does CutOut remove backgrounds?
One-tap removal on-device using Apple Vision, plus background replacement with built-in templates.

Are photos uploaded to your servers?
No. Processing happens entirely on your device.

Why does the app need Photos access?
To import images you choose and save results back to your library.

What does Pro add?
A one-time ¥3 (or local equivalent) unlocks unlimited cutouts, all backgrounds, and HD export (see in-app details).
