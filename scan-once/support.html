FAQ

Which languages does OCR support?
ScanOnce performs on-device OCR in seven languages. The supported set is listed in the app (and may be refined in updates). All recognition runs locally using Apple’s Vision framework—nothing is sent to our servers.

Can I scan multiple pages?
Yes. Pro unlocks batch scanning so you can capture and combine multiple pages. The free tier includes three scans total for you to try the workflow.

Where are my PDFs saved?
When you export a PDF, you choose the destination using the system share sheet (for example, Files, iCloud Drive, or another app). Scans and exports are not uploaded by ScanOnce; they go only where you save them.

Does ScanOnce need the internet?
No. Scanning, OCR, edge detection, and PDF creation work fully offline. The app does not send your documents over the network for those features.

I purchased Pro but features are still locked.
Open the app’s settings (or purchase screen) and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase.
