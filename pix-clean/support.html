FAQ

Will my photos be permanently deleted?
When you delete through PixClean, items are typically moved to the system Recently Deleted album (same as Photos). They remain recoverable for a period defined by Apple, then are permanently removed. You can restore early from Recently Deleted in the Photos app.

How accurate is duplicate detection?
On-device duplicate matching targets very high accuracy (about 97%+ in typical conditions) using Apple’s Vision framework. Edge cases—heavy edits, extreme crops, or different resolutions—may behave differently; always review suggestions before batch actions.

Does PixClean upload my photos?
No. All analysis runs on your device. Photos are never sent to our servers for duplicate, blur, screenshot, or large-file detection.

What’s included in the free version?
You get two library scans and access to the duplicates category. Pro unlocks unlimited scans, batch delete, and all four categories.

Pro didn’t unlock after purchase.
Tap Restore Purchases inside the app and ensure you’re signed in with the Apple ID that completed the purchase.
