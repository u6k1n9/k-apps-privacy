🧹 PixClean — Smart Photo Cleaner

PixClean

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes PixClean — Smart Photo Cleaner (“PixClean,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) and how we handle information. PixClean helps you clean your photo library by finding duplicates (using on-device analysis with high accuracy via Apple’s Vision framework), blurry photos (using Core Image Laplacian variance), screenshots, and large files. You can batch move unwanted items to the system “Recently Deleted” album.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. PixClean does not upload your photos, thumbnails, analysis scores, or library metadata to our servers. Your images are never transmitted by the app for analysis. We do not build user profiles, run cloud ML on your library, or use third-party analytics that access your photos.

On-Device Processing

Duplicate detection, blur estimation, screenshot identification, and large-file scanning run entirely on your device using Apple frameworks (including Vision and Core Image). Results are computed locally and presented to you in the app. Network connectivity is not required for this analysis.

Permissions Used

  • Photo Library — Required to read your library for analysis and to perform actions you approve, such as moving photos to “Recently Deleted.” PixClean only accesses photos as permitted by your iOS privacy settings.

Third-Party Services

PixClean does not embed third-party advertising, analytics, or photo-upload services. We do not sell your data.

In-App Purchase

Pro (USD $2.99 one-time, or local equivalent) unlocks unlimited scans, batch delete, and all four categories (duplicates, blurry, screenshots, large files). The free tier includes two scans and the duplicates category only. Payments are processed by Apple; we do not receive your full payment card number.

Children’s Privacy

PixClean is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children; the app does not send photo data to us.

International Data Transfers

We do not operate a service that copies your photo library to our computers. No photos leave your device for processing by PixClean’s analysis features. Any iCloud or device backup is under Apple’s and your account settings, not under our control.

GDPR Compliance (EEA, UK, and Similar Laws)

If you are in the EEA, UK, or a region with comparable laws: we do not process personal data on our own infrastructure through the core cleaning features, because analysis is local and we do not receive your images or library contents. Apple may process limited data related to in-app purchases. You may exercise GDPR-related rights with Apple where applicable. For questions about this policy, contact us at the email below.

CCPA / CPRA Compliance (California)

California residents: We do not sell or share personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA via PixClean. We do not collect personal information from the app into our databases. Photos analyzed on-device are not disclosed to us. See Apple’s privacy materials for purchase-related data.

Analytics

We do not use analytics or behavioral tracking in PixClean.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. The “Last updated” date will change accordingly. Check this page or App Store release notes for important updates.
