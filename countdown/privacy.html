📅 念日

Countdown - Date Tracker

Last updated: March 27, 2026

数据收集

念日（Countdown）不会将您的事件标题、日期或提醒内容上传至我们的服务器。所有纪念日、倒数与正数计时数据仅存储在您的设备本地。

权限说明

为在到期时提醒您，本应用可能请求「通知」权限。若您选择将事件加入系统日历，应用可能请求「日历」相关访问；该功能为可选，您可随时在系统设置中管理权限。

第三方服务

本应用未集成第三方分析、广告 SDK，亦不会将您的纪念日数据出售或提供给数据代理商。

应用内购买

您可选择一次性付费 ¥3 解锁 Pro，以获得不限数量事件、全部主题与小组件等功能。交易由 Apple 通过 App Store 完成。

儿童隐私

本应用并非面向 13 岁以下儿童设计，我们亦不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

数据分析

本应用不包含任何分析或统计追踪功能。

联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请发送邮件至：kapps.dev@outlook.com

English Version

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Data Collection

Countdown does not upload your event titles, dates, or reminder content to our servers. All birthdays, anniversaries, holidays, and countdown/count-up data stay on your device only.

Permissions

We may request Notifications so we can alert you when an event is due. If you choose to add events to the system calendar, the app may request Calendar access; this is optional and can be managed in Settings.

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate third-party analytics or advertising SDKs, and we do not sell or provide your event data to data brokers.

In-App Purchase

You may optionally purchase Pro for a one-time fee of ¥3 (or local equivalent) to unlock unlimited events, all themes, and widgets. Payments are processed by Apple via the App Store.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Analytics

This app does not include analytics or tracking.
