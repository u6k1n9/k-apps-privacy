Data Collection

Goals and related context you enter are stored primarily on your device. When you use features that rely on cloud AI to generate tough-love coaching content, the goal text and necessary context are sent over an encrypted connection to the DeepSeek API. We do not store that content or the AI requests/responses on our own servers. DeepSeek’s privacy policy and terms govern their handling of API data, including any retention; please review their official documentation.

Permissions

  • Notifications: For daily reminders and nudges related to your goals.

Third-Party Services

DeepSeek API: When AI-generated coaching runs in the cloud, relevant text is sent to DeepSeek. We do not persist that data on our infrastructure; DeepSeek’s security and policies apply while data is processed there. If you do not use cloud AI generation, your goal text is not sent to DeepSeek for that purpose.

In-App Purchase

A one-time purchase (¥3) unlocks Pro: all eight coach styles, unlimited daily roasts, and related features. Payments are processed by Apple; we do not receive your full payment card details.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. The tone may be blunt and is not recommended for young children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Analytics

We do not use analytics tools to track your usage.

Contact

For privacy questions, email kapps.dev@outlook.com
