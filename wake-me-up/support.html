FAQ

Where does my data go for AI-generated coaching?
Relevant text is sent to the DeepSeek API; we don’t store it on our servers. See the privacy policy.

What’s the difference between Pro and free?
A one-time ¥3 unlocks all eight coach styles and unlimited daily generations (see in-app details).

I’m not getting notifications.
Enable notifications for WakeUp in Settings and adjust Focus / Summary settings if needed.

The tone feels too harsh.
Try a different coach style in the app; stop using the feature or contact us if you have concerns.
