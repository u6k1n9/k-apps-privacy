FAQ

What does LEDBanner do?
It shows LED-style scrolling text for concerts, events, and cheering, using CSS animations and screen brightness control for visibility.

What’s the difference between free and Pro?
A one-time ¥1 (or local equivalent) Pro purchase unlocks more colors, effects, and shake-to-change features.

Is my text synced to the cloud?
No. Everything stays on your device.

Why is there a purchase?
Pro is an optional one-time in-app purchase processed by Apple; the free tier still offers core scrolling features (see in-app details).
