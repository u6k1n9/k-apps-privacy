数据收集

口袋工具不会在服务器上收集您的个人数据。二维码生成、单位换算、尺子、水平仪、分贝仪、取色、指南针、手电筒等工具均在设备本地运行；我们不会将相机画面、麦克风音频或您输入的换算数据上传至互联网。

权限说明

  • 相机：用于二维码扫描、取色等需要实时画面的功能。
  • 麦克风：用于分贝仪测量环境音量（音频在本地处理，不上传）。

第三方服务

本应用不向第三方上传上述传感器或相机数据用于分析或广告，也不集成以此为目的的第三方 SDK（以当前版本为准）。

Data Collection

PocketTools does not collect personal data on our servers. QR generation, unit conversion, ruler, level, decibel meter, color picker, compass, and flashlight features run locally on your device. We do not upload camera feeds, microphone audio, or your conversion inputs to the internet.

Permissions

  • Camera: For QR scanning, color picking from the live view, and similar features.
  • Microphone: For the decibel meter (processed on-device; not uploaded).

Third-Party Services

We do not upload sensor or camera data to third parties for analytics or advertising, and we do not integrate third-party SDKs for that purpose (as of the current version).

In-App Purchase

A one-time purchase (¥3) unlocks Pro, including access to five premium tools and related benefits—see in-app details. Payments are processed by Apple; we do not receive your full payment card details.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Analytics

We do not use analytics tools to track your usage.
