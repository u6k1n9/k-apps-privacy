FocusLock - Stay Focused

Last updated: March 27, 2026

数据收集

离手（FocusLock）不会将您的专注会话、统计数据或番茄钟记录上传至我们的服务器。所有计时与日历视图相关数据仅保存在设备本地。

权限说明

为在专注时段结束或需要休息时通知您，本应用可能请求「通知」权限。通知在本地调度，不依赖我们的远程服务器。

第三方服务

本应用未集成第三方分析、广告或云端同步服务，不向第三方分享您的专注数据。

应用内购买

您可选择一次性付费 ¥3 解锁 Pro，以使用全部模式（含番茄、自由计时、深度专注）、日历视图与详细统计等。交易由 Apple 通过 App Store 处理。

儿童隐私

本应用并非面向 13 岁以下儿童设计，我们亦不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

数据分析

本应用不包含任何分析或统计追踪功能。

联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请发送邮件至：kapps.dev@outlook.com

English Version

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Data Collection

FocusLock does not upload your focus sessions, statistics, or Pomodoro records to our servers. All timer and calendar-related data stays on your device only.

Permissions

We may request Notifications to alert you when a session ends or when it’s time for a break. Alerts are scheduled locally and do not rely on our remote servers.

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or cloud sync services, and we do not share your focus data with third parties for such purposes.

In-App Purchase

You may optionally purchase Pro for a one-time fee of ¥3 (or local equivalent) to unlock all modes (Pomodoro, free timer, deep focus), calendar view, and detailed stats. Payments are processed by Apple via the App Store.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Analytics

This app does not include analytics or tracking.

For privacy questions, email kapps.dev@outlook.com
