不会，所有数据仅保存在本机。

FAQ

What does SleepSound do?
It mixes white noise and ambient sounds for sleep, with 20 built-in sounds (bundled locally), up to 3 tracks, and a sleep timer.

Why does audio continue when the screen locks?
The app uses iOS background audio playback so listening can continue while you fall asleep.

What does Pro unlock?
A one-time ¥3 (or local equivalent) purchase unlocks all 20 sounds, full mixing, and the timer (see in-app details).

Are my mixes uploaded?
No. Everything stays on your device.
