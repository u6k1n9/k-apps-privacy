🌙 眠声

SleepSound - Ambient Mixer

Last updated: March 27, 2026

数据收集

眠声（SleepSound）不会将您的个人数据上传至我们的服务器。白噪音与环境音文件随应用本地打包；混音、定时器等设置均仅保存在设备本地。

权限说明

为在锁屏或切换应用后继续播放助眠音频，本应用使用 iOS 的「后台音频」能力（UIBackgroundModes: audio）。我们不会借此访问与音频无关的数据。

第三方服务

本应用未集成第三方分析、广告或云端音频服务，不向第三方分享您的使用数据。

应用内购买

您可选择一次性付费 ¥3 解锁 Pro，以使用全部 20 种声音、三轨混音与睡眠定时器等功能。付款由 Apple 通过 App Store 处理。

儿童隐私

本应用并非面向 13 岁以下儿童设计，我们亦不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

数据分析

本应用不包含任何分析或统计追踪功能。

联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请发送邮件至：kapps.dev@outlook.com

English Version

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Data Collection

SleepSound does not upload your personal data to our servers. Sound files are bundled with the app; mixer and timer settings stay on your device only.

Permissions

To keep playback running while the screen is locked or when you switch apps, the app uses iOS background audio (UIBackgroundModes: audio). We do not use this capability to access unrelated data.

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or cloud audio services, and we do not share your usage data with third parties for such purposes.

In-App Purchase

You may optionally purchase Pro for a one-time fee of ¥3 (or local equivalent) to unlock all 20 sounds, 3-track mixing, and the sleep timer. Payments are processed by Apple via the App Store.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Analytics

This app does not include analytics or tracking.
