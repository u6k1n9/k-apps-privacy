常见问题

AI 回信时我的信会被发到哪里？
文本会通过安全连接发送至 DeepSeek API 用于生成回复；我们不在自己的服务器上存储这些内容。详见本应用隐私政策中的「第三方服务」说明。

收不到签到提醒？
请在系统设置中开启每日平安的通知权限，并检查是否开启了专注模式或定时摘要。

Pro 包含哪些功能？
一次性 ¥6 解锁无限信件、AI 回信与全部模板等，具体以应用内说明为准。

换机后数据还在吗？
信件等数据主要保存在本机；若未使用 iCloud 或其它备份，换机后可能需重新创建。请以应用内实际行为为准。

FAQ

Where does my letter go when I use AI replies?
Text is sent securely to the DeepSeek API to generate a response; we don’t store that content on our servers. See the “Third-Party Services” section in the privacy policy.

I’m not getting check-in reminders.
Enable notifications for DailySafe in Settings and check Focus modes or Scheduled Summary settings.

What’s in Pro?
A one-time ¥6 purchase unlocks unlimited letters, AI replies, and all templates—see in-app details.

Will my data transfer to a new phone?
Data is stored primarily on-device; without backup, you may need to recreate content on a new device. Refer to in-app behavior.
