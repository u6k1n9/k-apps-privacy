数据收集

您撰写的家书、签到记录等主要内容默认存储在您的设备本地。为实现「AI 回信」功能，您选择使用该功能时，信件正文等文本会通过加密连接发送至 DeepSeek（深度求索）API，由对方模型生成回复内容；我们不会在自己的服务器上存储这些信件内容或 AI 请求/响应。第三方对请求数据的处理（包括是否短暂缓存、保留期限等）适用 DeepSeek 的隐私政策与用户条款，建议您同时查阅其官方说明。

权限说明

  • 通知：用于每日平安签到提醒等本地计划通知。

第三方服务

DeepSeek API：当您使用依赖云端 AI 的功能时，相关文本会传输至 DeepSeek 以生成回复。我们不在自有服务器上持久化存储上述内容；数据在传输与由 DeepSeek 处理期间，受 DeepSeek 安全措施与其政策约束。若您不使用 AI 回信类功能，则不会因此向 DeepSeek 发送您的信件文本。

应用内购买

您可选择一次性付费（¥6）解锁 Pro：无限信件、AI 回信、全部模板等。交易由 Apple 处理；我们不会收到您的完整支付卡信息。

儿童隐私

本应用并非面向 13 岁以下儿童设计或营销。我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。请监护人引导未成年人谨慎使用会向第三方 AI 发送文本的功能。

数据分析

我们不使用分析工具追踪您的使用行为。

English Version

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Data Collection

Letters you write and check-in data are stored primarily on your device. When you use features that generate AI replies, the text of your letter (and related prompt content) is sent over an encrypted connection to the DeepSeek API so a model can produce a response. We do not store that letter content or the AI requests/responses on our own servers. How DeepSeek processes, caches, or retains API data is governed by DeepSeek’s privacy policy and terms; we encourage you to review their official documentation.

Permissions

  • Notifications: For daily safety check-in reminders and similar local scheduled alerts.

Third-Party Services

DeepSeek API: When you use cloud AI features, relevant text is transmitted to DeepSeek to generate replies. We do not persistently store that content on our infrastructure; while data is in transit and processed by DeepSeek, DeepSeek’s security practices and policies apply. If you do not use AI reply features, your letter text is not sent to DeepSeek for that purpose.

In-App Purchase

A one-time purchase (¥6) unlocks Pro: unlimited letters, AI replies, all templates, and related benefits. Payments are processed by Apple; we do not receive your full payment card details.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Guardians should supervise minors’ use of features that send text to third-party AI services.

Analytics

We do not use analytics tools to track your usage.

Contact

For privacy questions, email kapps.dev@outlook.com
